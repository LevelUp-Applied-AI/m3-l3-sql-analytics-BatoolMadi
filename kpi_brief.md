# KPI Brief — Levant Tech Solutions

## KPI 1: Employee Distribution by Department

**Definition:**
Counts the number of employees assigned to each department.

**Current value:**
Engineering has the highest number of employees (12), followed by Customer Support (10). HR has the smallest team (5 employees).

**Interpretation:**
The company invests heavily in Engineering and Customer Support, suggesting a strong focus on product development and customer service operations. Smaller HR staffing may indicate limited internal administrative expansion.

---

## KPI 2: Average Salary by Department

**Definition:**
Calculates the average employee salary within each department.

**Current value:**
Engineering has the highest average salary (~78,166), while Customer Support has the lowest (~46,400).

**Interpretation:**
Higher salaries in Engineering reflect specialized technical skills and market demand. Lower averages in Customer Support align with operational roles that typically require fewer specialized technical skills.

---

## KPI 3: Organizational Resource Allocation Insight

**Definition:**
Comparison of workforce size and compensation across departments to evaluate company priorities.

**Current value:**
Technical departments (Engineering and Research) show both higher salaries and strong staffing levels compared to administrative departments.

**Interpretation:**
Levant Tech Solutions prioritizes innovation and product development. Investment patterns indicate a technology-driven business strategy focused on engineering excellence.
